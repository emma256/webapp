This is a community based organisation aimed at bringing together the youth in our community.

our objectives

  1. To sensitize our community about the need and importance of an educated community
  2. To promote acceptable social behaviour among the youth in our community
  3. To sensitize the youth about self sustainablity through setttig up projects in our community
  4. To give a helping hand to fellow students in need
  5. To prevent redundancy among the youth
  6. Sensitizingourcommunity with factual and dependable information abuot HIV/AIDS

Our mission "Transforming our community from ignorance to perception of modern ideas of living"
