history

Nyabihoko students association was founded by a group of visionary students of Nyabihoko parish in 1998 with thre aim of bringing together the of the community to a social understanding of working together to develop our community . After its formation it was fully registered as CBO and allowed officially to persue its objectives as laid in its constitution

administration

To achieve our objectives we need the best and organised management to spearhead the associations programs.Our admin is well organised to meet the goals of the group.

events

As one of our objectives to prevent redundancy among the youth and also to sentize our community about the need for an educated community plus advocation for an HIV/AIDS free community,we always set up through which we can deliver our message to the public. Therefore during the long holidays and festive season we hold different events such as;
  1. music dance and drama shows
  2. Games and sports competetions
  3. community work
